# Privacy Policy & Data Deletion Instructions

**App Name:** Tokyo Mini Theater Guide
**Developer:** Leo Nelki

## Privacy Policy
This application is a personal automation tool used solely to publish cinema showtimes to the developer's own Instagram account.

1. **Data Collection:** This app does not collect, store, or share any external user data.
2. **Data Usage:** The Instagram API is used strictly to upload media (images and captions) to the authenticated Instagram account.
3. **Third Parties:** No user data is transferred to third-party services.

## Data Deletion Instructions
Since this app does not store user data, there is no database to clear. However, if you wish to revoke the app's access to your Instagram account:

1. Open Instagram.
2. Go to **Settings** > **Apps and websites**.
3. Find "Tokyo Mini Theater Guide" (or your app name).
4. Click **Remove**.
