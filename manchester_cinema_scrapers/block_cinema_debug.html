Please note: Tickets are non-refundable. By purchasing a ticket, The Block Cinema grants admission to the Cinema and Film Club, to receive refreshments and dependent upon the event, to engage in discussion of the film. At all events, a film will screened. The film screened will be the one advertised. The ticket is for the purchase of the licence to enter the premises and take refreshments. The ticket price includes a free tea, coffee or soft drink and biscuits per ticket holder. Further refreshments must be purchased. When available and at the organisers’ discretion, the price will include popcorn or cake. The screening of the film is ancillary. The Block Cinema reserves the right to refuse admission or withdraw the licence for any breach of its rules and regulations and those of the Wythenshawe Community Housing Group. By purchasing tickets, you are agreeing to these terms and conditions.
