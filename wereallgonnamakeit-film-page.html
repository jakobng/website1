We're All Gonna Make It

The fallen angels of YouTube are dead, but their aesthetic gospel lives on.

About This Film

Set partly inside a working gym housed in a derelict church in Merseyside, the film explores how the pursuit of physical perfection has evolved into a kind of religion, one built on viral sermons, fallen angels, and the promise of salvation through discipline.
